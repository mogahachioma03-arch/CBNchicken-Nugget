    About Us

    CBN Chicken Nuggets is a brand built on quality, creativity, and unforgettable taste. We believe that great food should bring joy with every single bite. Our mission is to serve flavour-packed, exciting chicken meals that are anything but boring—meals that truly stand out in quality and satisfaction.

    Our journey began in 2019, right from our home kitchen. On February 14th, 2020, we made our first outdoor appearance, driven by a big dream to introduce a unique style of chicken meal to passionate chicken lovers. We started with our signature chicken nuggets, and over time, our passion and customer love helped us grow into a diverse menu that now includes Chicken Salad, Peppered Potatoes and Chicken, Chicken Shawarma, and many more delicious options.

    Every recipe is crafted with care, fresh ingredients, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

    At CBN Chicken Nuggets, we stand for freshness, quality, and customer satisfaction. We strive to be the go-to brand for every chicken lover seeking meals that are tasty, affordable, and truly memorable. Our mission remains the same: to deliver premium nuggets and chicken delicacies with world-class taste, one satisfied customer at a time.
